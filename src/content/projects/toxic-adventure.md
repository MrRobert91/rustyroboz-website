2d Plataform Game prototype

Simple platform game for android mobile phones. The objective is to collect 50 barrels of radioactive waste, the barrels appear randomly on the screen, and you have to go jumping to collect them, as you get barrels you go level (only 3 levels at the moment). It has very simple touch controls, a josystick to move and a button to jump, very simple menus that allow you to play again when your lives run out, and very basic sounds when jumping and obtaining the barrels. The backgrounds and the barrels are illustrations by: https://www.instagram.com/carlosbrayda/
