Short walking simulator game made for the Scream jam 2021 It was made over the course of 10 days, in Unity and it works on Windows, Linux and Android.

The game is a short, atmospheric experience in which the player explores a mysterious and unsettling world. The game was designed to be a "walking simulator" in which the focus is on exploration and discovery rather than on action or combat.
