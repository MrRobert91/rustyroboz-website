13 bullets in hyperspace

You have received a help message and decide to go investigate, it seems that the AI of a spaceship has become self-aware and the robots are out of control, and you only have 13 bullets to defeat them! And also something about getting a tesseract? Good Luck! Action VR game made in 9 days for the VRJam2022. You can try it in your Oculus Quest for free on itch.io:

https://drobert.itch.io/13-bullets-in-hyperspace
